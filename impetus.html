Impetus

Motivation for this website

Hi everyone! My name is Timothy Ogden, and I'm thrilled to share with you the story behind the study guide I created for our program.

As we all know, our coursework can be quite challenging, and it's not always easy to find the right resources to help you understand complex concepts or prepare for exams. When I was struggling with a particular subject, I found myself scouring the internet for study materials, only to end up with a hodgepodge of disorganized and sometimes unreliable information.

That's when I had the idea to create a centralized study guide tailored specifically for our program. I wanted to compile the most helpful resources, clear explanations, and practice questions in one place, making it easier for all of us to access the information we need.

But this project wasn't just about making my own life easier. It was about giving back to the community that has supported me throughout this journey. I know how much we all rely on each other for guidance, encouragement, and shared knowledge. By creating this study guide, I hoped to pay it forward and provide a valuable resource that could benefit everyone in our program.

The process of building the guide was a labor of love. I spent countless hours organizing, and editing the content to ensure it was as comprehensive and user-friendly as possible. I even reached out to some of our professors for their input and feedback, ensuring that the guide accurately reflected the course materials.

Now, with the study guide available to all of you, I hope it will serve as a valuable tool in your academic pursuits. Whether you're preparing for an exam, trying to understand a difficult concept, or simply looking for additional practice, this guide is here to support you every step of the way.

Remember, we're all in this together, and by supporting each other, we can all achieve greatness. Happy studying, and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or suggestions for improving the guide further!
